FAQs

What is ATS and why should I care?+
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software companies use to filter resumes before a human ever sees them. Over 75% of resumes get rejected by ATS because of bad formatting, missing keywords, or unreadable layouts. ResumePulse builds resumes specifically designed to pass ATS screening.
Is ResumePulse really free?+
Yes, 100% free. No hidden charges, no "premium" unlock, no watermark on your resume. We believe everyone deserves a shot at getting hired — not just people who can pay for resume builders.
How is this different from Canva or other resume builders?+
Canva and similar tools make visually stunning resumes — but ATS software can't read them. Multi-column layouts, icons, custom fonts, and graphics all break ATS parsing. ResumePulse uses a clean, single-column format that ATS reads perfectly, while still looking professional to human recruiters.
Can I download my resume as PDF?+
Yes! Once you fill in your details and preview your resume, you can download it as a clean, ATS-friendly PDF — ready to submit to any job portal.
Is my data safe?+
We don't store your resume on our servers beyond the session. Your data stays in your browser and is only sent to our backend for PDF generation. We never share, sell, or use your information for anything else. Check our privacy policy for the full details.
I'm a fresher with no experience. Will this work for me?+
Absolutely. ResumePulse is built with freshers in mind. The form lets you highlight projects, skills, and education — the things that matter most when you're starting out. You don't need work experience to create a strong, ATS-friendly resume.
